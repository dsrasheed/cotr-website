The Colors of the Region

Celebrating Racial Diversity in Northwest Indiana

About

SLYCE is a program created by the Southshore Leadership Center with the goal to develop gifted high school students into the future leaders of Northwest Indiana. Through community bus tours, informatative speakers, and staff of the SLYCE program, these high school students are inspired to undertake a mission to better the region.

The current group of high school students enrolled in the SLYCE program were particularly touched by a speaker named Katie Kirley. She came to speak about what she was doing to impact the community as the director of Steel City Academy, a school dedicated to providing the less fortunate kids of Gary access to a quality education. Consequently, the group of students decided to plan and host the Colors of the Region Event in order to both celebrate the diverse races, cultures, and ethnicities present throughout the region as well as serve as a fundraiser to Katie's school.

The event the students are hosting will be on June 30, 2017 at: Central Park, 68 Lafayette St, Valparaiso, IN. The event's attractions are three speakers, three bands, and the various food trucks and activites available to eat and play in the time between the performances and talks of the bands and speakers, respectively.
