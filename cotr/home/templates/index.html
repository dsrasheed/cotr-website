The Colors of the Region

Celebrating Racial Diversity in Northwest Indiana

About

The Colors of the Region Event is handled by the members of the 05 SLYCE class. Our goal is to raise awareness for the wide divsersity of races in the region through music, food, and other activities. All proceedings from this event will be given to Steel City Academy, a school in Gary dedicated to providing education to those who were born in unfortunate circumstances.
